Energy Forecasting

Deep learning for smarter energy management

Overview

Our energy forecasting project leverages long short‑term memory (LSTM) networks to analyse historical consumption data and predict future usage. By anticipating demand, organisations can optimise resource allocation, reduce operational costs and improve sustainability.

Business Benefits

  • Reduce energy waste through accurate consumption forecasts.
  • Improve budgeting and planning by anticipating demand peaks.
  • Support sustainability goals with data‑driven insights.
