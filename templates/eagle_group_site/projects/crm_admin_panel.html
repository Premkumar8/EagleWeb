CRM Admin Panel

Streamlined management of customer relationships

Overview

The CRM Admin Panel is a lightweight dashboard for managing customer data. Built with a focus on simplicity and responsiveness, it allows staff to add, update and remove customer records quickly and accurately. Designed to be self‑hosted, it brings all of your customer information into one intuitive interface.

Business Benefits

  • Centralise customer information for better visibility and decision‑making.
  • Improve team efficiency with an easy‑to‑use interface for adding and editing records.
  • Reduce administrative overhead by streamlining routine CRM tasks.
