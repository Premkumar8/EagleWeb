Knapsack Optimizer

Optimising resource allocation for maximum impact

Overview

The knapsack optimizer solves a classic resource allocation problem using dynamic programming. It selects the optimal combination of items—subject to a weight constraint—to maximise overall value. This technique underpins many real‑world decision‑making systems.

Business Benefits

  • Allocate budgets and resources efficiently across projects.
  • Maximise return on investment by prioritising high‑value tasks.
  • Streamline decision‑making with data‑driven optimisation.
