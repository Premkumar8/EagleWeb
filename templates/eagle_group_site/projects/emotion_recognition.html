Emotion Recognition

Understanding emotions through biosignals

Overview

This project explores the use of one‑dimensional convolutional neural networks to classify electrocardiogram (ECG) signals into distinct emotional states. By recognising how people feel in real time, businesses can tailor experiences, services and products to meet customer needs.

Business Benefits

  • Enhance user experiences by adapting to emotional feedback.
  • Improve customer satisfaction and retention through personalised interactions.
  • Gain insights into consumer behaviour and well‑being.
